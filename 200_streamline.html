Streamline






Streamline and app that curates content for example a company news letter
The company can then build a newsletter based off the subjects that they provide for the customers to select. Articles and photos are stored in the apps data base and are sent to the blast page which uses the backend logic to curate.
Using a mailing servicefor example Mandrill and a API key a comapany can email their subscribers content that only that user wants based off of selection the user makes that get stored into the database.
This is where the magic happens and the content is deployed to the massesJust push the Streamline Logo You can even check what is being deployed from this page before content is sent, how many articles being sent, and how many subscribers receiving content.
VOILA!! This is and real live email sent to a demo email address with only the content requested.
You can keep track of subscribers and e-mail adresses here as they are stored in the data base. There is even and option to CSV subscribers the company already has in a CSV file. clients can also be removed from this page.
